About Itachi Uchiha

Itachi Uchiha is a character from the manga and anime series Naruto. He is a member of the Uchiha clan and a former captain of the Anbu. Itachi is known for his calm demeanor, intelligence, and formidable ninja abilities. He is one of the most complex and beloved characters in the series, often depicted as a tragic hero with a deep sense of loyalty and sacrifice.
